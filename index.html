About Us

INTERIOR ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN
INTERIOR ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN

“We want designers to be afraid of the designers we foster”?

Interior Architectural design is concerned with the creation of the spaces in which we live, and the design of the interface between buildings and the people who use them.
It creates spaces full of encounter, responding to contemporary culture, anticipating change, entering meaningful dialogue with our past.
This course develops a strong appreciation of materials and making in context, giving you the practical skills needed to succeed in the industry.

VISION LAUNCH OFFERS FOLLOWING COURSES:-
  • Degree in Interior Design
  • Master Diploma in Interior Architectural design
  • Diploma in Interior Architectural design
  • Certificate course in Interior Architectural design
GRAPHIC DESIGN

“Graphics is the art of seeing the invisible”

The core skills of a graphic designer are rooted in language, narrative, typography and an understanding of markets, people, stakeholders and intentions.
Your graphic design work can impact on economic, political, social, cultural and personal life.

VISION LAUNCH OFFERS FOLLOWING COURSES IN GRAPHIC DESIGN:-
  • Certificate course in Graphic Design
  • Diploma in Graphic Design
  • Advance diploma in Graphic Design
